Community For Riders

Cycling Without Age is built on the concept that we can create a greater sense of community through the shared experience of a bike ride. During a ride, seniors and their “pilots” can share stories and build relationships. This is especially important for seniors in the Tenderloin, where breaking social isolation is necessary. Through this program, something as small as a bike ride can remind isolated seniors of their youth and build connections with their neighbors
